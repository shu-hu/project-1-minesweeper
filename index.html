Minesweeper

HOW TO PLAY
Game Instruction
  • How to Start?
  • Choose a level to start game, then you are able to click any square on the board.
  • What happend after click?
  • If you click a square contains a bomb, you lose the game.
  • If you click a square without bomb, then a number will display on the board.
  • The number on the board represent how many bombs are adjacent to this square.
  • You are also able to right click a square to mark this square.
  • The number of flags equals the total bombs in the board.
  • How to Win?
  • To win the game you have to click all the squares with no bombs.
