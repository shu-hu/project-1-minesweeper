Minesweeper

HOW TO PLAY

Game Instruction

How to Start?

Choose a difficulty level to start a game.

How to Play?

You can click on any square to reveal it.

If the square contains a bomb, you lose the game immdiately.

If the square does not contain a bomb, then a number will display on top of the revealed square.

The number on the square represents the number of bombs that are adjacent to this square.

You are also allowed to right click a unrevealed square to mark it as potentially dangerous.

The number of available flags equals to the total number of bombs on the whole board.

How to Win?

To win the game, you have to click/reveal all safe squares that do not contain bombs.
